Whats my Goal?

My goal in life is to be able to help all those who may not have the luxuries I have whether I end up as a billionaire or just live a modest life. Growing up I had the luxury of being exposed to a lot of diverse individuals and it made me realize that I was very fortunate to have what I had. Some people had it much worse than me. Some families lived pay check to pay check. It made me realize the importance of appreciating what I have in life and doing my job to try and help people that I share this world with.
